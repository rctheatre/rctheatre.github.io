Matilda the Musical

"Matilda the Musical" is a captivating stage adaptation of Roald Dahl's beloved children's novel, brought to life with whimsy, wit, and a touch of magic. Set in the heart of a darkly humorous world, the musical follows the extraordinary journey of Matilda Wormwood, a precocious young girl armed with intelligence, courage, and an unyielding spirit. With an enchanting score by Tim Minchin, the production unfolds as Matilda navigates the challenges of family, school, and self-discovery, ultimately triumphing over adversity and inspiring audiences of all ages with its empowering message of resilience and the transformative power of imagination.

The Production Team

Here is some information about our wonderful production team that you'll get to work with.

Producer: Joshua Cluff

Josh comes from a long heritage of theatre. He wishes he could audition for Bruce Bogtrotter but is excited to bring the magic of Matilda to the Rexburg community.

Director: Sadie Steel

Sadie is excited to bring the story of Matilda to life with the help of an incredible production team! She loves to take part in anything that can uplift and build others, especially through the arts.

Choreographer: Annabelle Hibbard

Annabelle has been in theatre and dance her whole life, adding choreography to the mix in her college days. She is excited to work with the wonderful members of the community to bring to life the meaning of Matilda.

Stage Manager: Lizzy Pingry

Lizzy loves the back-stage tech life! She has stage managed or run tech for several shows and loves working with the amazing production team and actors.

Assistant Stage Manager: Austin Griggs

Music Director: Megan Scott

Megan is thrilled to be working with RCT again! Music has been a part of her life since she was big enough for her mama to make her stand up and sing for all the events. She is so excited to have the opportunity to connect with other wonderful people through Matilda the Musical!

Assistant Music Director: Holly Slack

Holly has been involved with RCT for the past 12 years as an actress and vocal director. She is super excited to once again work with such awesome and talented people.

    • Audition Information

    Welcome to the auditions for "Matilda the Musical"! We are excited you are here and to announce that auditions for our upcoming production will be initially held digitally this year. In person auditions will be by invitation ONLY and will be held at the VFW. Please review the information below for details on how to prepare for your audition and how to submit your digital audition.

    Callbacks - Live Auditions

  • Callbacks will be held in person on March 19, 20. You will be notified by email if you are called back. If you are called back, you will be given a specific time to attend.
    • March 19: 6:00-9:00 PM (If invited to this audition, all music sides and script sides will be provided. If you are invited for a further dance callback you will be notified to dress appropriately).
    • March 20: 6:00-9:00 PM (If invited to this audition, all music sides and script sides will be provided. If you are invited for a further dance callback you will be notified to dress appropriately).
  • Participation fees will only be collected once the cast has been set in order to simplify the process. If you drop after the show is cast, you will forfeit all fees.
  • All live auditions will be held at: VFW (Veterans of Foreign Wars), 480 W 2nd N, Rexburg, Idaho
