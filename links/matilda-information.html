Matilda the Musical

"Matilda the Musical" is a captivating stage adaptation of Roald Dahl's beloved children's novel, brought to life with whimsy, wit, and a touch of magic. Set in the heart of a darkly humorous world, the musical follows the extraordinary journey of Matilda Wormwood, a precocious young girl armed with intelligence, courage, and an unyielding spirit. With an enchanting score by Tim Minchin, the production unfolds as Matilda navigates the challenges of family, school, and self-discovery, ultimately triumphing over adversity and inspiring audiences of all ages with its empowering message of resilience and the transformative power of imagination.

    • Audition Information

    Welcome to the auditions for "Matilda the Musical"! We are excited you are here and to announce that auditions for our upcoming production will be initially held digitally this year. In person auditions will be by invitation ONLY and will be held at the VFW. Please review the information below for details on how to prepare for your audition and how to submit your digital audition.

    Audition Restrictions

  • All children auditioning must be 8 years old by the performance dates (June 21, 22, 24)
  • Children 8-17 MUST have parent or guardian signature on the Google Audition form
  • All initial auditions (digital) MUST be submitted online via the Google Form provided by MARCH 12 at 11:59pm. No Exceptions.

    • Digital Auditions

    Digital auditions for Matilda will consist of a vocal submission in video form as well as a dance submission video. There will be no monologue required for the digital audition.

    Vocal Submission

    Please prepare 16-32 bars of a vocal selection in the style of the show. Please DO NOT choose a song from the show. Be sure your selection shows off both your vocal ability in the range you are comfortable in and your storytelling abilities. Be sure the vocals can be heard clearly above any recorded or live accompaniment. The vocal audition must be recorded live. We do not want old choir concerts or show performance recordings submitted. DO NOT exceed a 1 minute recording for your vocal audition submission.

    Dance Submission

    Please submit a recording of you performing the dance steps provided in the dance step tutorial found below. Be sure to be dressed appropriately and have plenty of room to perform the steps. The recording should give us a full view of the person performing the steps. Please be aware, we are looking for a group of experienced dancers to cast as well as ensemble members who will be performing less intense dance steps.
