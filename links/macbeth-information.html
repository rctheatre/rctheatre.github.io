Macbeth

Ambition. Betrayal. Murder. Shakespeare's Macbeth is a thrilling descent into the darkness of the human soul. A brave warrior, Macbeth receives a tempting prophecy: he will be king. Fueled by ambition and urged on by his relentless wife, he takes fate into his own bloodstained hands. But power comes with paranoia, and murder leads to more murder. Experience the epic battle between a man's desire for greatness and the gruesome consequences of unchecked ambition. This haunting world of witches, ghosts, and a ruthless thirst for the crown awaits those daring to enter.

Join Rexburg's second annual Shakespeare in the Park production for a thrilling night of outdoor theater. Witness the drama and power of Shakespeare's work come to life on stage.

The Production Team

Here is some information about our wonderful production team that you'll get to work with.

Producers: Sam and Jesse Head

Sam and Jesse started Rexburg’s first production of Shakespeare in the Park last year with a comical production of A Midsummer Night’s Dream. They are excited to help continue this tradition in our community and branch out to one of Shakespeare’s tragedies.

Director: Lucas Buchanan

Summer 2024 - Shakespeare in the Park: Macbeth

Show Information

Performance Dates: July 5, 6, and 8, 2024 @ Porter Park’s Beehive Pavilion stage. Performances will be at 7:00pm, with a pre-show starting at 6:30pm

Rehearsals: Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday evenings from 6:00-9:00 pm beginning Tuesday, May 14 Additional rehearsals will be added as needed on Saturday mornings during the month of June.

Location: All performances and rehearsals will be held in Porter Park at or near the Beehive Pavilion stage, Rexburg, Idaho.

Audition Information

Auditions will be held April 20 and 27 from 3-6pm. To prepare, we ask that you memorize and perform 1-2 minutes of a Shakespeare monologue. Also be prepared to do cold reading of selections of the script with other auditioners during your audition time.

Role Descriptions

Participation fees ($35) will only be collected once the cast has been set in order to simplify the process. If you drop after the show is cast, you will forfeit all fees.

All auditions will be held at Madison High School’s Performing Arts Center, 2300 University Blvd, Rexburg, ID 83440 (please enter through the back)
