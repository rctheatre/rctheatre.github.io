A Night of One Acts

Join us for an unforgettable evening of creativity and community as Rexburg Community Theatre showcases a collection of captivating one-act murder mistory plays! This unique event promises hilarious comedy and eyebrow raising curiosity, all presented by local talent. Every ticket supports our efforts to obtain our own venue, ensuring that the arts continue to thrive in our community. Come for the entertainment, stay for the cause, and help us build a brighter future for local theater!

The Production Team

Here is some information about our wonderful production team that you'll get to work with.

Director: Rob Hibbard

Director: Annabelle Hibbard Summers

Annabelle has been in theatre and dance her whole life, adding choreography to the mix in her college days. She is excited to work with the wonderful members of the community to direct one of these act murder mystery.
