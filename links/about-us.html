About Us

Rexburg Community Theatre is a nonprofit organization focused on providing professional, quality, affordable family theatre. Our goal is to enhance the lives of both those onstage and audience participants. RCT is committed to include all persons regardless of gender, race, or special needs.

A bit of history

The concept of Rexburg Community Theatre started in 2005 when Ann Cluff met Rob Hibbard. This dynamic duo had a vision to bring quality, affordable family theatre to the Rexburg and surrounding areas that would be fueled by the community. They wanted to provide a place where people could come together and enjoy the arts both on and off the stage. After a few years of planning and organizing, RCT was born in 2007. Since then, RCT has produced over 30 shows and has become a staple in the community.
